MY AMARETTO SOUR

-1 1/2 oz Amaretto

-3/4ish oz Burbon

-1ish oz lemon or lime juice (depends what I've got)

-2 little spoons of cherry surup

-1 egg white

Put everything in a shaker with no ice and shake it vigorously. The eggwnite will expand and you'll probably end up making a mess but it doesn't foam right otherwise. Then shake it all up with ice and serve. Garnish with citiris rind and merechino cherries.
